About Me

Hello! I’m Alison, a student at Binghamton University studying graphic design, economic analysis, and mathematical sciences. I love learning, creating, and finding ways to help make the world a better place.

Born and raised in New York City, I have a deep interest in NYC culture, politics, and change. One of my main sources of this information is The New York Times. A loyalist to The Times, when I’m not busy reading op-eds or about NYC news, you can find me reading their “Modern Love” or “Metropolitan Diary” sections.

Right now, I’m not quite sure what graduating means for me. In many ways it seems like a mysterious void that I find myself pondering more often than I should. With that, I hope you enjoy following me along the journey!

Thank you for stopping by!

– Alison :)
